What is Scholarly Capital?

Scholarly Capital is the name of our project for this event. It's a website that students can sign up for to get statistics on their finances.

How does it work?

When registered for the site, it asks for an email, a username, and a password. Once you're finished, you can start using the site. The site handles, processes, sorts and outputs a student's financial information.

Why would someone want to use it?

This site allows for an easy and efficient way to manage your money and make good financhial decisions.
